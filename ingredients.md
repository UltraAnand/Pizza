#Ingredients for Paneer Makhni


##For Curry


Water – ½ cup
Tomato diced – 5 cups
Onion sliced – ½ cup
Garlic cloves – 8 no
Ginger – 1 small piece
Bay leaf – 1no
Black cardamom – 1no
Cinnamon – 1 stick
Cardamom – 3no
Cloves – 3 no
Green chillies – 1-2 nos
Kashmiri chilli powder – 1 tbsp
Butter – 1 tbsp
Cashew nuts – 12nos
Salt – to taste

##On Pan


Butter – 2 tbsp
Ginger chopped – ½ tbsp
Green chilli – 1 no
Paneer cubes – 2 cups
Salt – to taste
Kashmiri chilli powder – 1 tsp
Dried Fenugreek (methi) leaves – ½ tsp
Sugar – 1-2 tsp (optional)
Cream – 3-4 tbsp
